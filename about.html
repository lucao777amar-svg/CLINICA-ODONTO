About LuminaDent

Two decades of compassionate, expert dental care in the heart of New York.

Who We Are

A Legacy of
Dental Excellence

LuminaDent was founded in 2004 with a single mission: to provide world-class dental care in a space where patients feel genuinely at home. Over two decades, we've grown into a multi-specialty practice with a team of 12 dedicated specialists.

We believe exceptional dentistry is more than technical skill—it's listening, empathy, and treating every patient as an individual.
